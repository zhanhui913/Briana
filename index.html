ABOUT

With experience in print, film and digital media, Briana is a multidisciplinary Graphic Designer with an eye for detail, and a passion for perfection. Post college, she honed her craft in the deadline driven environment of print design, production and editing, and became an ace at customer and team relations. A volunteer designer with the Asian Film Festival and the International Bhangra Festival, Briana enthusiastically looks for opportunities to contribute her skills and energy as part of a creative team.
